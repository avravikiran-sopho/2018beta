About Us

Elan & Nvision, the most anticipated and grandiose festival of IIT Hyderabad, is back with a conglomerate of splendiferous events to galvanize the mob. Since inception, its fervid tendrils have touched the hearts of every adroit organiser and participant leaving a legacy to be upheld by its family of dedicated students.

Elan and nvision is an elegant brew of culture, innovation and feverish exhilaration stimulating an atmosphere of intense competions and professional performances. With a carefully fabricated range of events, covering varied genres in art, music and technology, we have something for everyone.

Elan & Nvision 2018, arguably the grandest techno-cultural fiesta you will ever come across, promises an eximious experience for spectators and participants alike.
