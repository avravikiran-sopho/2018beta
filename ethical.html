About


Elan & ηvision, IIT Hyderabad in association with Azure Skynet presents to you a workshop on Ethical hacking and Cyber Security.
No prerequisites required.


Venue : IIT Hyderabad
Date : 26th and 27th August
Timing : 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM
Fee per person - ₹ 1000/-

Highlights


  • Hacking strategies and methods
  • Google hacking, Web hacking and countermeasures
  • Social networking sites protection
  • Get trained by professionals
  • Certificate from Elan & ηvision, IIT Hyderabad
  • Hacking tools software to each student
  • Live Demos and Interactive Question & Answer sessions
